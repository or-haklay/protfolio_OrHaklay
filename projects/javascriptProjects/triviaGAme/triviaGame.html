Game Rules:

  • Choose a Category – Select one of the available trivia categories before starting the game.
  • Answer 10 Questions – You will receive 10 random questions from your chosen category.
  • Time Limit – You have 60 seconds to answer each question.
  • Four Answer Choices – Each question has one correct answer and three incorrect options.
  • No Changing Answers – Once you select an answer, you cannot change it.
  • Scoring System – You earn 1 point for each correct answer.
  • Game Over – The game ends when all 10 questions are answered or time runs out.
  • Final Score – Your total score will be displayed at the end of the game.
  • Play Again – You can restart the game and try to beat your previous score.
  • Have Fun! – Enjoy the challenge and test your knowledge!
